# Chocolate-Sales-Analysis
Multi-year sales performance analysis using Power BI and DAX.

## Objective
Analyze multi-year chocolate sales data to evaluate revenue trends, identify key growth drivers, and investigate performance volatility across countries and product categories.

## Dataset
The dataset includes:
- Sales revenue
- Units sold
- Country
- Product
- Transaction date (2015–2024)

## Key Analysis Performed
- Year-over-Year (YoY) revenue growth analysis
- Quarter-over-Quarter (QoQ) performance comparison using DAX time intelligence
- Country-level revenue contribution assessment
- Product-level performance breakdown
- Investigation of Q4 2024 revenue decline

## Key Insights
- Across the three-year period, sales consistently increase from Q1 to Q2 before declining through Q4. This pattern is observed across all regions, suggesting seasonality or internal operational factors or incomplete reporting rather than market-specific influences.
- Revenue distribution appears well balanced, with no significant concentration in a small number of countries or products, indicating a diversified performance portfolio.
- Australia remains the leading revenue contributor throughout the period, while Smooth Silky Salty consistently ranks as the top-performing product.

## Business Implications
- Revenue growth is dependent on high-performing markets and key product lines.
- The Q4 2024 anomaly warrants further investigation into inventory levels, supply chain activity, or data completeness.

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- DAX (Time Intelligence Functions)
- Data Modeling
- SQL (Data Preparation / Querying)
- Excel
